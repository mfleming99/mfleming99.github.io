Electric Skateboard - Video Link

This is my electric skateboard. It has a 190kv brushless sensored motor and a total of 10s, 5000 mah batteries. It is controlled by a wiresless wii nunchuk. I used a VESC from Enertion to control the motor. The VESC is capable of IC2 communication, so the reciever of the wii nunchuk can connect directly to the VESC. Initially I had a lower quality ESC, which needed an arduino to communitcate with the recieve. The direct connection works much more reliably. I made the 4s battery out of a faulty 6s battery. Everything is attached into the tupperware using velco.

Drink Dispencer - Video Link

This robot dispences drinks based off of users voice input. I used an app called Android Meets Robot, made by ____. The Android meets Robot app prossesses the speech into text, and sends it to the bluetooth reciever I put in the drink dipenser. The bluetooth reciever then sends the text to the arduino clone. The arduino clone can prosseess the text and then decide what to do. There we a couple diffrent commands that the user could tell the robot. In the video I said, "Make me a drink." which dispences 1.5oz of the red fluid and 4.5 oz of the green fluid. To make this robot better, I could have used a voice recognition hardware-based speech recognition module, so I wouldn't have to use a phone. I also would have used more powerful motors, as the motors were only capable of pumping 3oz a minute.
